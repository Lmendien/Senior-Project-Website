Hall of Fame:

DALE MARTIN

Greenville, IL
Email Address: dfmcrm@juno.com
  1. What years did you quiz?
    1955-56, 1956-57, 1957-58
  2. Where and for what church did you quiz?
    Winona Lake (IN) Free Methodist Church
  3. What were the highlights of your quizzing years?
    Attending Youth Camp at Camp Mack in northern Indiana; meeting other quizzers; having Florence Murray, a retired missionary, as our coach.
  4. Did you or your team win any championships? If so, what and when?
    We were 3rd in 1956, quizzing on Romans. In 1957 (first year the electric chairs were used) we won the championship on the book of John. We were runners-up (by 15 points) in 1968, quizzing on Genesis.
  5. What was your favorite scripture? Why?
    At this distance in time, I can't recall a favorite.
  6. How did quizzing affect you as a teen?
    Quizing gave a focus to our summer, and helped develop concentration and dedication, spurred on by the challenge of competition.
  7. What difference has quizzing made in your life to this day?
    It has given me a high regard for scripture and its importance, as well as an understanding of the value of memorization so that the Word is close at hand.
  8. Are you presently involved in Bible quizzing in any way or have you since you were a quizzer? If so, how?
    A couple of our kids participated when they were in FMY.
  9. What ministries are you currently doing? How are you presently active in serving the Lord?
    I've taught at Greenville College for 38 years and am active in our local FM church.
